# Pan Samochodzik - Feature Ideas

This document outlines potential features for implementation in the Pan Samochodzik automotive platform.

## Vehicle Management System
- **Description**: Allow clients to add and manage their vehicles
- **Key Components**:
  - Vehicle registration (make, model, year, VIN, registration number)
  - Vehicle details and specifications
  - Maintenance schedule tracking
  - Service history per vehicle
- **User Value**: Clients can manage multiple vehicles and track their complete service history in one place

## Appointment Scheduling System
- **Description**: Calendar-based appointment booking system for mechanic services
- **Key Components**:
  - Calendar for mechanics to set available time slots
  - Appointment booking interface for clients
  - Confirmation and reminder notifications
  - Rescheduling and cancellation features
- **User Value**: Streamlined scheduling process for both clients and mechanics

## Service Records & History
- **Description**: Comprehensive system for tracking all vehicle services
- **Key Components**:
  - Detailed service records (parts, labor, costs)
  - Service history timeline
  - Service report generation
  - Invoice generation and tracking
- **User Value**: Complete transparency and documentation of all work performed

## Mechanic Profile & Specialization
- **Description**: Enhanced profiles for mechanics to showcase their expertise
- **Key Components**:
  - Specialization fields and certifications
  - Rating and review system
  - Portfolio of completed repairs
  - Experience and background information
- **User Value**: Helps clients find mechanics with specific expertise for their needs

## Automotive Marketplace
- **Description**: Platform for parts listing and purchasing
- **Key Components**:
  - Parts catalog with search functionality
  - Integration with suppliers
  - Quote request system
  - Order tracking
- **User Value**: One-stop shop for automotive service and parts needs

## Vehicle Diagnostics Tracking
- **Description**: System to record and track vehicle diagnostic information
- **Key Components**:
  - Diagnostic code recording and explanation
  - Maintenance recommendations based on diagnostics
  - Potential integration with OBD readers
  - Historical tracking of issues
- **User Value**: Better understanding of vehicle health and required maintenance

## Cost Estimation Tool
- **Description**: Tool for mechanics to provide detailed service estimates
- **Key Components**:
  - Itemized parts and labor quote creation
  - Client approval workflow
  - Conversion from estimate to invoice
  - Comparison with actual costs
- **User Value**: Transparency in pricing and service costs

## Workshop Management
- **Description**: Tools for mechanics to manage their workshop operations
- **Key Components**:
  - Workshop profile and details
  - Service areas and specialties configuration
  - Parts inventory management
  - Tool and equipment tracking
- **User Value**: Enhanced operational efficiency for mechanics

## Notification System
- **Description**: Comprehensive notification system for the platform
- **Key Components**:
  - SMS/email notifications for appointments
  - Service reminders based on mileage or time
  - Status updates on ongoing repairs
  - Custom notification preferences
- **User Value**: Keeps users informed without requiring them to check the platform

## Client-Mechanic Chat System
- **Description**: Secure messaging system between clients and mechanics
- **Key Components**:
  - Text-based messaging
  - Photo/document sharing
  - Voice/video consultation options
  - Message history and search
- **User Value**: Direct communication channel for questions, updates, and consultations

## Implementation Considerations

When selecting features for implementation, consider:

1. **User Value**: Which features provide the most immediate value to users?
2. **Technical Complexity**: What is the development effort required?
3. **Integration**: How well does the feature integrate with existing functionality?
4. **Market Differentiation**: Which features help distinguish the platform from competitors?

Each feature should be evaluated against these criteria before implementation prioritization.
